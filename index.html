Why we charge

SuperPlex used to be free.

For a long time, this was a passion project — shared freely with friends and family. But as the library grew to 40TB across thousands of titles, so did the costs of running it.

Servers, hard drives, power bills, and bandwidth add up fast. The small monthly fee is what keeps everything online, fast, and growing — with no ads and no compromises.

SuperPlex will never have ads. Your $10 is what makes that possible. Every dollar goes directly into storage and infrastructure — nothing else.

Requests

Can't find it?
Request it.

Seerr is where you request anything missing from the library. Search, tap request, done — most content arrives within a few hours. You can also save it as an app on your phone for quick access.

1

Open Seerr & sign in

Visit requests.superplex.org and sign in using the credentials provided in your Discord approval message.

2

Browse or search

The home screen shows genres, upcoming movies, and popular series. Or use the search bar at the top to find anything specific. If it shows a green tick, it's already in the library — just open Plex to watch it.

3

Request what's missing

Find a title that's not available yet? Tap it and hit Request. It goes straight into the queue and you'll get notified when it's ready. Most content arrives within 1–6 hours.

📲

Save it as an app

Android automatically prompts you to install Seerr as an app — just tap Install when you see the banner. On iPhone, tap the Share button in Safari and choose Add to Home Screen. Works like a native app from then on.
